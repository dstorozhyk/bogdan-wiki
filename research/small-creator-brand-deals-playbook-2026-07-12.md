# Small Creator Brand Deals Playbook

Practical synthesis from a NotebookLM research notebook created for the **«Все про Royal»** project. Related: [[small-creator-brand-deals-pricing-2026-07-12]].

## Positioning for a small creator

Early-stage creators should initially manage brand work themselves: own outreach, negotiation, deadlines, contracts, publishing and reporting. A manager becomes worthwhile when inbound volume and negotiation/operations materially exceed the value of the typical manager commission. The research session cited a commonly discussed **20%** commission, but this is a market heuristic—not a universal rule—so any agreement needs direct review.

## First-deal workflow

1. Select 3–10 brands whose customers closely match the channel’s audience.
2. Build a short media kit: niche/elevator pitch, audience demographics, recent median views and engagement, prior examples, and conversion evidence where available.
3. Identify influencer-marketing or partnerships contacts, primarily through LinkedIn; use Instagram DMs only to obtain the appropriate business contact.
4. Warm the relationship with relevant organic mentions where editorially appropriate.
5. Send a concise value-led pitch: relevance to the brand, authentic fit, proof of audience response, and an easy executable concept.
6. Agree deliverables, timeline, review process, disclosure, price, payment terms, usage rights and category exclusivity before production.
7. Publish with the required advertising disclosure; send live links and a short post-campaign report/invoice.

## Commercial guardrails

- Sell a business outcome and audience fit, not subscriber count alone.
- Separate the base integration fee from paid-ad usage rights, exclusivity, rush delivery, extra revisions, and extra distribution formats.
- Do not accept affiliate-only compensation by default; a fixed fee plus trackable commission can align incentives for suitable offers.
- Record outcomes via unique promo code, UTM link, inquiries, bookings, sales, and screenshots/partner confirmation.
- Preserve editorial trust: decline irrelevant or dubious products even when the fee is attractive.

## 30-day starting plan

- **Days 1–7:** choose target verticals/brands; prepare media kit, case-study format and outreach tracker.
- **Days 8–20:** publish relevant organic content; collect contact names; capture baseline audience and performance metrics.
- **Days 21–30:** send personalised pitches; follow up systematically; log each answer, offer and outcome.
