# Small Creator Brand-Deal Pricing — «Все про Royal»

Related: [[small-creator-brand-deals-playbook-2026-07-12]]. This is a working pricing hypothesis from session `20260712_155402_433d3750`, not a market quote card.

## Core conclusion

A channel with approximately **2,000 stable YouTube views** should not justify a high integration price solely with CPM or subscriber count. However, a verified conversion case can justify outcome-based pricing: the project reportedly brought **10 confirmed participants** to an author-led European tour after a recommendation.

For relevant travel or premium offers, that conversion evidence supports a **4,000–5,000 UAH** opening price for a 45–60 second YouTube native integration, subject to proof, audience fit and deliverables. The value argument is qualified demand—not raw views.

## Suggested structure

- Base YouTube native integration: **4,000–5,000 UAH** when the conversion case is documented and relevant.
- Larger integration with link/promo code: **5,000–7,000 UAH**.
- Multi-platform package (YouTube, TikTok, Telegram): **7,000–10,000 UAH**, only after each channel’s current performance is checked.
- Travel/high-consideration offers: fixed base plus a trackable per-booking or revenue-share component where reporting is credible.

## Do not bundle for free

Quote separately for paid-ad usage rights, category exclusivity, rush delivery, extra revision rounds and additional platform distribution. Include unique tracking identifiers in every deal.

## Validation checklist before sending a rate card

- [ ] Calculate median—not best-case—views for the last 10–20 videos over 30 days.
- [ ] Capture audience geography, age, engagement and topical fit.
- [ ] Document the tour case: campaign period, recommendation format, 10 confirmed bookings, partner permission, and any available attribution evidence.
- [ ] Define each deliverable and usage right in writing.
- [ ] Compare offers only against relevant local niche creators; do not rely on generic CPM benchmarks.

## Caveat

The tour result was reported in conversation and has not been independently verified by this consolidation job. Use it only after confirming the attribution and permission to disclose it.
